## 📂 Featured Projects

### 🎮 [Video Game Market Analysis](https://github.com/inafgs/Video-Game-Market-Analysis)
> Full data analysis pipeline on 16,000+ historical video game sales records. Identified platforms, genres, and regional markets with the highest commercial potential using statistical hypothesis testing (scipy).

`Python` `pandas` `numpy` `matplotlib` `seaborn` `scipy`

---

### 🚗 [Vehicle Listings EDA Dashboard](https://github.com/inafgs/Vehicle_Listings_EDA_Dashboard)
> Interactive web application to explore and visualize a U.S. car sales dataset. Non-technical users can uncover patterns in odometer distribution and the correlation between mileage and sale price through user-triggered visualizations.

`Python` `Streamlit` `Plotly` `EDA`

---

### 📡 [Telecom Plan Revenue Analysis](https://github.com/inafgs/Telecom-Plan-Revenue-Analysis)
> End-to-end pipeline integrating 5 independent datasets to identify the most profitable prepaid plan. Applied Welch's t-test to statistically confirm revenue differences and support commercial budget decisions.

`Python` `pandas` `numpy` `matplotlib` `seaborn` `scipy` `hypothesis testing`
